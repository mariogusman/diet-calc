Daily Calory Counter

This is a simple calculator that can help you estimate your ideal daily caloric intake based on your current stats and goals.

This is known as a TDEE calculator which stands for Total Daily Energy Expenditure and is very important when working on weight loss or muscle gain programs, aligned with exercise a healthy lifestyle and with your Doctor's support.

Please note this is for educational purposes only and you should always consult with a doctor before making any changes to your diet or exercise routine.
