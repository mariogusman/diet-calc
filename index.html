Daily Calorie Calculator

This is a simple calculator that will help you estimate your ideal daily caloric intake based on your current stats and goals.

We will calculate your BMR will be calculated using the Harris-Benedict equation which will be used to estimate your TDEE using the Katch-McArdle multipliers.

Note that the results will change once your stats change. We recommended that you re-calculate this at least once a month or when noticeable changes occur.
